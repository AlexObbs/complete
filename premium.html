7-Day Premium Package Itinerary

Embark on an 7-day journey through the heart of Africa's wilderness. Experience breathtaking wildlife encounters, immersive cultural experiences, and luxurious accommodations in this carefully curated Luxury package.

Day 1

Arrival in Nairobi.

VIP Welcome: Guests are greeted at Jomo Kenyatta International Airport with champagne, a welcome kit (including premium Kenyan tea and handmade souvenirs), and a dedicated concierge.
Activity: Private transfer to a 5-star city hotel. Enjoy an optional spa session or visit the Karen Blixen Museum for a personalized after-hours tour.
Accommodation: Palatial suite at Giraffe Manor or Hemingways Nairobi.
Highlight: Dinner at a fine-dining restaurant with panoramic views of Nairobi National Park.

Day 2

Nairobi to Maasai Mara (Chartered Flight)

Morning: Take a private chartered flight to the Maasai Mara, landing at an exclusive airstrip near your luxury lodge.
Afternoon: Begin your safari with a private game drive in a fully outfitted 4x4 Land Cruiser. Sip on curated cocktails while spotting wildlife.
Value Addition: Private sundowner setup in the savannah with a Maasai guide to narrate local legends.
Accommodation: High-end safari lodge or tented camp, such as Angama Mara or Mahali Mzuri, offering panoramic Mara views.
Highlight: Tailored evening meal under the stars with a personal chef.

Day 3

Full-Day Maasai Mara Safari

Morning: Start the day with an unforgettable hot air balloon safari over the Mara, followed by a champagne breakfast in the bush.
Afternoon: Exclusive guided safari with a professional wildlife photographer for personalized tips and photo opportunities.
Evening: Private cultural interaction with Maasai elders and a traditional dance performance at the lodge.
Accommodation: Same luxurious camp/lodge as Day 2.
Highlight: Gourmet dining paired with rare Kenyan wines.

Day 4

Maasai Mara to Amboseli (Flight)

Morning: Fly directly to Amboseli National Park on a private charter. Enjoy an aerial view of Mount Kilimanjaro during the journey.
Afternoon: Arrive at your lodge, where you’ll be treated to a refreshing welcome drink and a light gourmet lunch.
Evening: Embark on a golden hour game drive to see Amboseli’s iconic elephant herds and other wildlife.
Accommodation: Stay at a luxury eco-lodge such as Tawi Lodge or Amboseli Serena Safari Lodge, offering unparalleled views of Kilimanjaro.
Highlight: Private bush dinner with a live pianist.

Day 5

Full-Day Amboseli Exploration

Morning: Guided walking safari led by expert naturalists, offering a closer connection to Amboseli’s flora and fauna.
Afternoon: Relax at the lodge with a private poolside massage or yoga session. Value Addition: Exclusive behind-the-scenes tour with conservationists working to protect Amboseli’s elephants.
Accommodation: Same as Day 4.
Highlight: Sundowner cocktails in a scenic location with a view of Kilimanjaro.

Day 6

Amboseli to Nairobi & Lake Naivasha

Morning: Fly back to Nairobi for a gourmet brunch, then travel via private luxury vehicle to Lake Naivasha. Stop en route for curated wine tasting at a premium Kenyan vineyard.
Afternoon: Arrive at a private lakeside retreat for an afternoon of relaxation.
Evening: Enjoy a sunset boat ride to see hippos and exotic birdlife. Accommodation: Stay at a high-end lodge, such as Loldia House, overlooking Lake Naivasha.
Highlight: Dinner featuring locally sourced ingredients paired with a fireside ambiance.

Day 7

Departure

Morning: Return to Nairobi for a farewell shopping spree at high-end Kenyan artisan boutiques (e.g., Kazuri Beads or Matbronze).
Afternoon: Guests enjoy a farewell lunch at the iconic Carnivore Restaurant or Tamarind Nairobi.
Evening: Private airport transfer with VIP lounge access before departure.
Highlight: Guests leave with a premium photo book documenting their trip and curated keepsakes.
