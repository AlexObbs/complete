7-Day Saver Package Itinerary

Embark on an 7-day journey through the heart of Africa's wilderness. Experience breathtaking wildlife encounters, immersive cultural experiences, and luxurious accommodations in this carefully curated Luxury package.

Day 1

Arrival in Nairobi.

Arrival: Guests arrive at Jomo Kenyatta International Airport and are welcomed by their assigned guide.
Check-in: Transfer via local transport (Uber or Matatu) to an affordable, comfortable BnB in Nairobi or an audited homestay.
Activity: Relax and acclimate. Optional evening walk to a nearby market for local crafts and souvenirs.
Dinner: Authentic Kenyan meal at a local eatery or with the host family.
Highlight: First taste of Kenya’s warmth and hospitality.

Day 2

Nairobi Exploration

Morning: Visit the David Sheldrick Wildlife Trust to see baby elephants being cared for.
Midday: Lunch at a budget-friendly café serving Kenyan delicacies.
Afternoon: Head to the Giraffe Centre to interact with endangered Rothschild giraffes.
Evening: Experience a bustling local market, such as Maasai Market (days vary), for a true taste of Kenyan culture.
Highlight: Interaction with wildlife and local artisans.

Day 3

Journey to Ololokwe in Laikipia

Morning: Travel via public bus or private matatu to Laikipia.
Afternoon: Arrival and check-in at a simple yet scenic BnB or audited homestay near Ololokwe.
Activity: Guided hike up Ololokwe, a sacred mountain offering breathtaking views.
Evening: Relax with locals over a casual dinner.
Highlight: Stunning landscapes and cultural immersion.

Day 4

Cultural Day

Morning: Spend time with a local community to learn traditional skills like beadwork or farming techniques.
Afternoon: Participate in a barbecue night with locals, featuring Nyama Choma (grilled meat), chapati, and other Kenyan favorites.
Evening: Storytelling session by the fire.
Highlight: Deep cultural connections and unforgettable culinary experiences.

Day 5

The Great Maasai Mara

Morning: Travel via local transport to Maasai Mara. Check in to a budget-friendly tented camp.
Afternoon: Guided game drive to witness the Big Five and other wildlife.
Evening: Enjoy a simple meal while soaking in the Mara's tranquility.
Highlight: World-class wildlife adventure on a budget.

Day 6

Maasai Mara & Return to Nairobi

Morning: Sunrise game drive followed by breakfast. Late Morning: Visit a Maasai village to learn about their traditions and buy handmade souvenirs.
Afternoon: Return to Nairobi using local transport.
Evening: Rest or optional night tour of Nairobi city.
Highlight: Combining wildlife with Maasai culture.

Day 7

Farewell and Free Time

Morning: Free time for last-minute shopping or a visit to Karura Forest for a nature walk. Afternoon: Pack and prepare for departure. Evening: Airport transfer for their journey home. Highlight: Relaxed conclusion with optional activities.
