Book Your Dream Tour in 6 Easy Steps

Planning your perfect adventure has never been easier. Follow these simple steps to secure your spot on our amazing tours.

1

Explore Our Packages:

Visit our website, Kenyaonabudgetsafaris.co.uk, and browse our range of packages (Saver, Standard, Premium, or Luxury and family package). Each package is designed to suit various budgets, preferences, and travel styles and groups

2

Contact Us for Personalization Once you’ve selected a package or if you need guidance:

Fill out the Contact Form on the website or use the provided WhatsApp/email option. Share details about your:Preferred travel dates. Group size (solo, family, friends). Special interests or requests (e.g., dietary requirements or extra activities). If you want a fully tailored experience, we can create a customized itinerary based on your needs.

3

Confirm Package Details:

After discussing your requirements:
We will send you a detailed itinerary, including accommodation, transportation, activities, and costs. Review the itinerary and suggest any adjustments before finalizing.

4

Make Payment:

Once you’re satisfied with the itinerary:
Secure your booking by making a deposit (specifics will be shared with you). Payment methods include bank transfer, online payment platforms, or PayPal. After payment, you’ll receive a confirmation receipt and booking reference.

5

Receive Travel Documents:

Before your trip, you will receive:
A detailed travel guide with tips,travel insuarance, contact details of your guide/driver,visa, and emergency information. A copy of your personalized itinerary.

6

Arrival in Kenya:

Upon arrival, our team will be at the airport to welcome you. Start your adventure with a dedicated guide and pre-arranged transportation/accommodation.
