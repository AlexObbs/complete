7-Day Luxury Package Itinerary

Embark on a luxurious 7-day journey through Kenya, exploring iconic landscapes, vibrant wildlife, and rich cultural heritage. Perfect for families, couples, and adventurers seeking an exclusive safari experience, this premium package blends opulent accommodations, curated activities, and unforgettable encounters with Kenya’s beauty. Highlights include the Maasai Mara, Lake Nakuru, and personalized cultural interactions.

Day 1

Arrival in Nairobi.

Welcome Experience: Upon arrival, enjoy a personalized meet-and-greet service. Guests receive a welcome kit with Kenyan snacks, a local SIM card, and a small souvenir.
Activity: Depending on arrival time, visit the Karen Blixen Museum or the Giraffe Centre.
Accommodation: Luxury boutique hotel in Nairobi with a fine dining dinner.

Day 2

Nairobi to Maasai Mara

Morning: Depart for the Maasai Mara in a private 4x4 vehicle, stopping for scenic views of the Great Rift Valley.
Afternoon: Arrive at your luxury tented camp in time for lunch. Evening: Embark on a guided sunset game drive, spotting the Big Five in Kenya’s premier wildlife reserve.
Value Addition: Extended safari hours at no extra charge.
Accommodation: Luxurious Maasai Mara camp with private terraces overlooking the savannah.

Day 3

Full-Day Maasai Mara Safari

Morning & Afternoon: Full-day game drives to witness wildlife, including cheetahs, lions, and elephants. Visit the Mara River for a chance to see crocodiles and hippos.
Value Addition: Enjoy a cultural tour of a Maasai village with traditional dances and the opportunity to participate in beadwork crafting.
Optional: Upgrade to a hot air balloon safari for breathtaking aerial views of the Mara.
Accommodation: Luxurious Maasai Mara camp.

Day 4

Maasai Mara to Lake Nakuru

Morning: Drive to Lake Nakuru National Park, renowned for its flamingos and rhinos.
Afternoon: Check into your luxury lodge and enjoy a gourmet lunch before heading out for a game drive. Spot endangered species such as the Rothschild giraffe and white rhinos.
Evening: Relax with a complimentary spa treatment.
Accommodation: Eco-lodge near Lake Nakuru with stunning lake views.

Day 5

Lake Nakuru to Amboseli National Park

Morning: Travel to Amboseli National Park, famed for its elephant herds and iconic views of Mount Kilimanjaro.
Afternoon: After checking in, enjoy a leisurely afternoon game drive.
Value Addition: Exclusive photo session with a professional photographer.
Accommodation: High-end lodge with panoramic views of Kilimanjaro.

Day 6

Full-Day Amboseli Safari

Morning & Afternoon: Explore Amboseli’s unique ecosystems, capturing elephants, flamingos, and zebras set against the backdrop of Africa’s tallest mountain.
Optional: Add a guided bushwalk or night safari for a rare wildlife experience.
Evening: Savor a cultural dinner with live traditional music.
Accommodation: Luxurious lodge in Amboseli.

Day 7

Departure

Morning: Return to Nairobi for a farewell lunch at a top-rated restaurant.
Optional: Visit the David Sheldrick Elephant Orphanage before airport transfer.
Farewell: Guests are gifted a curated photo album with highlights of their journey.
